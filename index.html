Secure Landed Property Rights (SLPR)

Our SLPR service ensures the protection of landed property through:

  • Legal verification and due diligence.
  • Comprehensive title insurance coverage.
  • Conflict resolution frameworks for property disputes.
  • Integration with public and private cadastral systems.

We also provide guidance on bureaucracy, governmental influence, and judiciary oversight to help clients navigate complex property systems.

Zero Tolerance Trespass Prevention Checklist

  1. Verify the legal ownership of the property.
  2. Install visible boundary markers and fencing.
  3. Maintain documented proof of ownership.
  4. Engage local authorities for monitoring.
  5. Promptly address unauthorized access or disputes.
  6. Maintain historical records of any trespass or claims.
